About CorMetels Metals

CorMetels Metals started as a Tool and Die shop in 1982, in the United Kingdom. After four decades of service, our values have remained the same. We take pride in treating customers right the Lou-Rich way! We maintain a positive, challenging workplace and a healthy work-life balance. Sustain a safe and secure working environment through a collaborative culture.


We broaden our employee job skills with professional development, education, and training while embracing innovation and technology in delivering exceptional performance for our customers. We operate with social responsibility to our community and the environment.
